        Notes

        The data used here comes from community research and may not match the actual in-game values.

        If a Luck Cap shows as MAX, raise your luck level to get closer to the optimal chance. The value in parentheses is the chance at a luck level near that cap.

        If you have an Items Farming build at 25,000 luck and a Luck build at 100,000 luck and the difference is tiny, pick the higher capacity build (Items Farming) and give up a small percentage.
